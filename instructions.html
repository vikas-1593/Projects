Instructions for the game.






  • Connecting Dots is a game for two persons.
  • Both players have 21 identical men.
  • One set of men is yellow and other set is red.
  • Yellow has got the first move.
  • The game is played on a vertical,rectangular board consisting of 7 veritcal columns of 6 squares each.
  • If a man is put in one of the columns,it will fall down to the lowest unoccupied sqauare in the column.
  • As soon as a columns contain 6 men,no other man can be put in the column.
  • The players make their move in turn.
  • Both players will try to get four connected men,either horizontally,vertically or diagonally.
  • The first player who achieves one such group of four connected men, wins the game.
